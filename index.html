WHO WE ARE

Better Comunication E.I.R.L. is a telecommunications company based in Huancayo, at the heart of Peru’s Mantaro Valley. We specialize in the sale of new mobile devices from a wide range of trusted brands, all backed by factory warranties and expert technical support.

VISION

To be the leading company in the regional market for smartphone sales, recognized for offering the best variety of brands, technological innovation, and exceptional customer service.

MISSION

We offer our customers a wide selection of smartphones from the best brands, guaranteeing quality, competitive prices, and personalized service that meets their communication and connectivity needs.
